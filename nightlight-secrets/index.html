Some exciting new stuff is coming!

Discover the new branding!

We're really happy to announce that Suicide For Him is getting rebranded Nightlight Secrets!

Futhermore, the game has been entirely rebuilt from scratch for a better optimization.

Q&A

WHAT THIS CHANGE MEANS?

Don't worry, the game is still the same as you remember! All the characters, the story, backstory... will remain the same. This is mostly nothing more than a major update with a brand new name.

WHAT IF I ALREADY PURCHASED SUICIDE FOR HIM?

If you already purchased the game as Suicide For Him, then no worries! The game will simply get updated in your Steam library from Suicide For Him to Nightlight Secrets and you will continue to receive the updates for the upcoming acts like before.

WHY "NIGHTLIGHT SECRETS"?

As told, we're very careful about our players feedback. We noticed that the name Suicide For Him wasn't very appropriated, and it wasn't really reflecting how the game changed over the past years. Nightlight Secret, the new game's name, is now the new name to reflect the mysterious secrets of the story and also the dreams and nightmares you are able to play as Akai. This new name change will also allow us to attract a younger player base too, meaning that the rating will be re-ajusted as well.

WHEN WILL IT RELEASE?

It is currently too early to announce when this major change will release. We know you are desperately waiting for a new update since the first release in November 2021, and we are really sorry about the long wait... However, please don't forget that this project is being developed by a really small team on their free time. As of now, you can still get the old Suicide For Him through Steam like before, and as said above you will receive the rebranding update as soon as we are ready to release it!
